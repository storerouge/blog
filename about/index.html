    About

    We present news and feature stories about people—alumni, faculty, and students. We report on University events, history, research, and scholarship. And we focus on matters of general interest that shape and are shaped by members of the Fordham community in New York City and throughout the world. The print edition (circulation 194,000) is published twice a year, in late fall and late spring. Alumni begin receiving the magazine after graduation. Parents of current Fordham students also receive the print edition. Our monthly email newsletter includes stories from the print edition plus timely news and features found only at
